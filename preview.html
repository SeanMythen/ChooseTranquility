Tension and stress are two natural forces that affect the body and our overall health. Stress is a part of everyday life. Many things can trigger stress, it varies from individual to individual, and each person has a different built-in response to stress.

To decrease the effects of stress on the body and mind, one of the relaxation techniques I use is vibrational sound which induces relaxation. Contact me to continue your journey of tranquility.

The system of relaxation I use is designed to elicit the relaxation response through multiple channels, increasing its effectiveness, and shortening the overall time between the base stress level and the relaxation response point. The unique thing about this relaxation technique is that once the client becomes familiar with the process, the relaxed state can become more pronounced and induced more quickly.
