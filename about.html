Hi, I’m Sonia and I am here to help you relax.

I’m a Registered Yoga Teacher and a Certified Practitioner of Vibrational Sound Therapy.

Vibrational Sound Therapy is a method of deep relaxation that can relieve stress and improve emotional well-being. It combines vibration and sound to induce an immediate relaxed state. A session is similar to a massage, but you remain fully clothed and the only areas of contact are bowls being placed on the body.

I use the Measured Strike Technique to create waves that penetrate deeply into tissues and provide you with a greater sense of relaxation. This technique produces a long lasting sound, with an even volume and consistent vibration throughout the session. It is not vigorous, but the waves are powerful and the process is effective.

The system of relaxation I use is designed to elicit the relaxation response through multiple channels, increasing its effectiveness, and shortening the overall time between the base stress level and the relaxation response point. The unique thing about this relaxation technique is that once the client becomes familiar with the process, the relaxed state can become more pronounced and induced more quickly.
